About Horizon

Small enough to know you.

Most assisted living facilities operate at scale. Horizon doesn't. We're a licensed, Hennepin County–based home that serves just five residents at a time — which means everyone on staff knows every resident personally. Days here have a gentle rhythm: morning walks, quiet afternoons, shared meals. We handle the care; residents handle the living.

Services

Care that is customized to you.

Every resident at Horizon has a care plan built around them — not around a schedule. Our primary service is 24-Hour Customized Living, Minnesota's gold standard for assisted home care.

24-Hour Customized Living

Around-the-clock, personally designed care in a licensed residential setting. Staffed every hour so help is always nearby — not on a call schedule.
