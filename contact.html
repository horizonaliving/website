What to expect on a tour

Tours take about 30–45 minutes. You'll see the resident rooms and common areas, meet on-duty staff, and have time to ask questions. We welcome family members and care coordinators. Bring a list of questions — the longer, the better.

Working with a case manager?

If you're working with a county case manager or care coordinator, we can communicate directly with them on your behalf. Let us know in your message and we'll coordinate from there.
